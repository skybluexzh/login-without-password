* This Demonstration Software including two parts:

	1.JavaChat
	2.JavaChat_Android

* The SDK verstion is Android4.4.2.

* Make sure you have MYSQL installed in your computer. Add the chatdb.sql file to your database.

* Add MySQL-connector-Java-5.1.32 to your JavaChatbuild path Libaries for Database connection.

* Change the IP to your own localhost IP.The spots are listed below:

ChatFrame.java, line #173(in JavaChat's chatclient packege) MainActivity.java, line #86(in JavaChat_Android file) sendPhoto.java, line #74(in JavaChat_Android file)

* Lunch Steps:
1.launch chatserver in JavaChat
2.launch chatclient in JavaChat
3.launch JavaChat_Android
